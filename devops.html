Execution Rules

protect depth
Rule 1

Deploy something real

DevOps only becomes meaningful when you host an actual tool or service.

Rule 2

Keep fundamentals tight

IAM, compute, storage, DNS, and logging are the core. Master these first.

Rule 3

Use networking as your edge

Your networking background should make cloud infrastructure easier, not harder.

Rule 4

Production starts small

Logging, security hygiene, and visibility matter even for small deployments.
